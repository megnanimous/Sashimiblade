PROJECTS

 
 
 
 

Komoot

  • The #1 outdoor app in Germany, Austria, and Swizerland. With our app, pros and enthusiast hikers, bikers, and runners alike can easily discover the best places to go outdoors. download detailed offline maps and navigate your way to and from awesome highlights with voice navigation. Currently working on just about every aspect of the app with an awesome team in Berlin!
  • View in the App Store: DE   EN

Be Brave

  • Help raise awareness for childhood cancer with this fun photo app! Take pictures, simply put an overlay on them and share on your favorite social media network to show your support for a great organization helping families cope with the financial burden of caring for their child. All app sale proceeds go to helping out families in the Orlando, FL area.
  • Organization web site: Be Brave Foundation
  • View in the App Store
  • App promotional page

Hapto

  • Simple vacation time calculator and tracker (and my first app in the App Store). Determine how much vacation time you have available on a given date, accounting for recorded vacation time. Figure out how much time it will take to bounce back from a negative PTO value.
  • View in the App Store

Legacy

  • Explore the timelines of prominent historical figures and view their related Wikipedia page. Links with your Facebook to explore what someone accomplished at your exact age, to the day!

  • View in the App Store

Yumlist

  • Link your Yummly or Pinterest boards to YumList and microblog your culinary creations! Add notes, pictures, and more to the recipes you have added on Yummly or Pinterest. Work in progress, source available on Github
